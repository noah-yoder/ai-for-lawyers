# AI for Lawyers

Public course site for **AI for Lawyers**, a J.D. elective co-taught at IU McKinney School of Law by Noah Yoder and Nick Merker. Hands-on training in practicing law *with* AI, paired with the doctrine — and the professional-responsibility duties under ABA Formal Opinion 512 (2024) — that govern it.

**Live site:** https://noah-yoder.github.io/ai-for-lawyers/

## What's here

- **`index.html`** — the course overview / landing page
- **`admin/`** — catalog description and pre-course setup
- **`labs/`** — weekly lab handouts and the synthetic exercise documents students work from
- **`capstone/`** — the capstone scenario menu and scoping worksheet

## A note on the materials

Every client, matter, party, and document in the exercise files is **synthetic** — invented for teaching. Any resemblance to real people or matters is coincidental.

Instructor-only materials (answer keys, grading rubrics, and teaching notes) live in a separate private repository and are intentionally not published here.

## Status

This is an early, deliberately malleable proof of concept. Structure and content will change.
